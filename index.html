Who We Are

Built by field experience,
run with discipline.

MACS Construction is a full-service general contractor serving commercial, industrial, and civil infrastructure clients. From initial site assessment through final inspection, our team combines hands-on field expertise with rigorous project management to keep every job on schedule, on budget, and to code.

We believe good construction is built on three things: careful planning, a safety-first culture, and people who show up and do the work right. That approach has guided every project we've delivered, and it's the standard we hold ourselves to on every site, every day.

  • Licensed, bonded, and insured across state projects
  • OSHA-aligned safety program with dedicated compliance staff
  • In-house engineering, estimating, and project controls
